## v1.2.0 — Deliverables, Document Search & Engagement UX

*Released July 9, 2026*

### Deliverables

- Deliverable stage roles and permissions — control who can move a deliverable through each stage.
- Due date events for deliverables — track deadlines directly on the engagement timeline.
- Folder health insights with penalty scoring and expanded metrics for deliverable folders.

### Document Search

- Global document search across every client and engagement you have access to — search by content, topic, or filename from a single panel.
- Firm-level search with authentication — search scoped to your firm with secure access control.
- Client and engagement filters in the search panel for narrowed results.

### Engagement UX

- Share status redesign with overview metrics in the engagement management view.
- Mentions tab in engagement comments — filter comment threads where you are tagged.
- Dual navigation on comment tiles — open the comment thread or jump directly to the document.
- Drag-and-drop reordering in the Shares tab with improved tooltip display for long document names.
- Tab navigation feedback with an indeterminate progress bar on tab click across all workspace levels.
- Files tab navigation now driven exclusively by deeplink URLs — eliminates stale breadcrumb state on refresh.
- Folder upload modal redesign with improved UX and progress feedback.
- Document IDs now visible in the file list and Shares tab for easier cross-referencing.

### Fixes

- Drive access revoked automatically when a member is removed from an engagement.
- Reminder cleanup on engagement close and member removal.

## v1.0.0 — Firma: Firm-Scale Client Portal on Your Google Drive

*Released May 20, 2026*

General Availability release of Firma — a non-custodial client portal built on top of Google Drive for consultants, boutique agencies, and fractional leaders.

---

### Core Portal

- **Bring Your Own Google Drive** — non-custodial; files never leave your Drive
- **Firm → Client → Engagement** hierarchy with branded client portal
- Persona-based access with 4 engagement roles (firm admin, engagement lead, client, external collaborator)
- No per-seat charges — unlimited internal and external users on paid plans
- Google OAuth 2.0 integration; no IT admin permissions required

### Engagement Management

- Active engagement limits by plan: Standard (10), Pro (25), Business (50), Enterprise (100)
- One-click engagement closure
- Document comment threads for feedback and approvals
- Engagement activity audit trail — 30 days on Standard, unlimited on Enterprise
- Document version history — 30 days on Standard, unlimited on Enterprise

### Advanced (Pro+)

- Custom subdomain, document versioning, engagement & document templates
- Advanced review & approval workflows
- Document access tracking

### Business & Enterprise

- Self-destruct timers & Never Share tags
- Automated follow-ups & reminders
- Custom DNS domain and SSO/SAML (Enterprise only)
- SLA-based priority support with named contact (Enterprise only)

### Support

- Dedicated in-app Support Portal on all paid plans — submit tickets, track status, threaded comments
