## v1.0.0 — Firma: Firm-Scale Client Portal on Your Google Drive

*Released May 20, 2026*

General Availability release of Firma — a non-custodial client portal built on top of Google Drive for consultants, boutique agencies, and fractional leaders.

---

### Core Portal

- **Bring Your Own Google Drive** — non-custodial; files never leave your Drive
- **Firm → Client → Engagement** hierarchy with branded client portal
- Persona-based access with 4 engagement roles (firm admin, engagement lead, client, external collaborator)
- No per-seat charges — unlimited internal and external users on paid plans
- Google OAuth 2.0 integration; no IT admin permissions required

### Engagement Management

- Active engagement limits by plan: Standard (10), Pro (25), Business (50), Enterprise (100)
- One-click engagement closure
- Document comment threads for feedback and approvals
- Engagement activity audit trail — 30 days on Standard, unlimited on Enterprise
- Document version history — 30 days on Standard, unlimited on Enterprise

### Advanced (Pro+)

- Custom subdomain, document versioning, engagement & document templates
- Advanced review & approval workflows
- Document access tracking

### Business & Enterprise

- Self-destruct timers & Never Share tags
- Automated follow-ups & reminders
- Custom DNS domain and SSO/SAML (Enterprise only)
- SLA-based priority support with named contact (Enterprise only)

### Support

- Dedicated in-app Support Portal on all paid plans — submit tickets, track status, threaded comments
