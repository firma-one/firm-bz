# Firma GTM Research & Recommendations

## 🧠 1. What You’re Building (Core Thesis)

**Firma — a client portal + engagement workspace for high-touch service businesses**

**Core Idea:**
A structured, client-facing layer on top of cloud storage (Drive/OneDrive) for:

* Deliverables
* Communication
* Engagement visibility
* Trust-building

**Key Insight:**
This is not just a “portal” → it’s a **perception + workflow product**

---

## 🎯 2. Your Target ICP (Refined)

### Primary ICP (Best Starting Wedge)

**Fractional Executives (especially Fractional CMOs)**

**Why this works:**

* High-ticket clients
* Manage multiple clients simultaneously
* Deliverables-heavy role
* Need to appear premium
* Currently use fragmented tools

---

### Secondary ICPs (Later Expansion)

* Strategic advisors / consultants
* Boutique agencies
* Audit / compliance firms
* Implementation partners

---

## 🔥 3. Core Problem You’re Solving

### Workflow Fragmentation

* Google Drive + Notion + Email + WhatsApp + Slides
* No unified client experience
* Poor presentation of work

---

### Perception Problem (Critical)

Clients don’t clearly see the value of the work.

**Symptoms:**

* Scattered delivery
* Lack of narrative
* Low visibility
* Reduced trust

---

### Internal Pain

* Repeated status updates
* Version confusion
* Manual reporting
* No standardized engagement structure

---

## 💡 4. Product Positioning

### Current (Weak)

* “Client portal”
* “Engagement workspace”

---

### Recommended Positioning

**Option 1:**

> Make your client work visible, structured, and premium

**Option 2:**

> The client-facing layer for your consulting business

**Option 3 (Best for CMOs):**

> Show clients the value of your work—without more meetings

---

## 🧭 7. GTM Strategy

### 1. Go Hyper-Narrow

Target only:

> Fractional CMOs managing 3–10 clients

---

### 2. Focus on a “Moment of Value”

**Key Moment: Deliverable Presentation**

* Strategy docs
* Campaign plans
* Performance reports

Position Firma as:

> How you present your work to clients

---

### 3. Nail One Killer Use Case

**Primary Use Case:**

> Monthly Marketing Report Portal

Replace:

* Static PDFs
* Email attachments

With:

* Live workspace
* Structured insights
* Continuous narrative

---

### 4. Create a Strong POV

Examples:

* “If your work is not visible, it is undervalued.”
* “Consultants lose deals due to poor presentation, not poor work.”

---

### 5. Programmatic SEO

Target:

* client reporting for fractional CMO
* marketing strategy presentation
* agency client portal examples
* marketing report templates

---

### 6. Content Strategy

Focus:

* Before vs after workflows
* Real examples
* Critique of bad delivery practices

**Sample Topics:**

* Why clients ignore your strategy docs
* How reports reduce perceived value
* Why Google Drive is hurting your business

---

### 7. Distribution Channels

**Primary:**

* LinkedIn

**Secondary:**

* Twitter (build-in-public)
* Founder-led demos

---

### 8. Demo Strategy

**Don’t demo features**

Instead:

* Show transformation
* Show client experience

---
